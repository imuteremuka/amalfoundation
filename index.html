About Us

We are a humanitarian and Charity Organization based in Malawi and have been operating since 2010 and officially since 2019. We help you in fulfilling all your religious duties through charity work, charity distribution and continuous / ongoing charity (sadaqatul jariyah).

We work with different organizations and companies that we share same ideologies and vision with. Our main goal is to ease your duty in fulfilling what we are obliged to do as human beings towards our fellow humanity.
