About Us

We are a humanitarian and Charity Organization based in Malawi and have been operating since 2010 and officially since 2019. We help you in fulfilling all your religious duties through charity work, charity distribution and continuous / ongoing charity (sadaqatul jariyah).

We work with different organizations and companies that we share same ideologies and vision with. Our main goal is to ease your duty in fulfilling what we are obliged to do as human beings towards our fellow humanity.

Our Programs

We are committed to making a lasting impact through our various programs

Mosque Construction

Building places of worship to serve communities and provide spiritual guidance.

Water Solutions

Borehole drilling and solar installations to provide clean, sustainable water sources.

Relief Aid

Ramadan food hampers and emergency relief for those in need.

Women Empowerment

Programs to support and uplift women through education and skills training.

Orphan Care

Supporting orphans with education, healthcare, and basic needs.

Qurbani & Aqiqah

Facilitating religious sacrifices and distribution of meat to those in need.
